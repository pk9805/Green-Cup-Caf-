About Us

Green Cup Café was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to serve exceptional coffee while minimizing our environmental impact. We source our beans directly from sustainable farms, use biodegradable packaging, and actively participate in local environmental initiatives.

Our café is more than just a place to grab coffee – it's a community hub where people can connect, work, and relax in an eco-conscious space.
